A restyled look inspired by the Android 12+ / HyperOS lock screen:
* Clock and date tinted from your wallpaper on Android 12+ (Material You); older versions stay white
* Clock and date in the MiSans lock-screen font; calendar in Google Sans
* Calendar events shown as solid, Google-Calendar-style rounded color blocks
* Removed the calendar icon and left column; the list snaps to whole blocks (no clipped rows) and stays scrollable
* Tapping the clock now opens whichever clock/alarm app you have installed, instead of a single hard-coded one
